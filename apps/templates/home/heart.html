Exercises

Brisk Walking

A simple and effective exercise, brisk walking can help improve cardiovascular health, lower blood pressure, and reduce the risk of heart disease.

Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact aerobic exercise that strengthens the heart and improves circulation, while also being gentle on the joints.

Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that helps increase cardiovascular endurance and strengthens the heart and lungs.

Interval Training

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) involves alternating between intense bursts of activity and short periods of rest. It has been shown to improve heart health and increase overall fitness.

Strength Training

Incorporating resistance exercises like weightlifting or bodyweight exercises helps build muscle mass and boost metabolism, leading to improved heart health.

Diet Plan

Fatty Fish

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fish like salmon, mackerel, and trout can help reduce inflammation, lower triglycerides, and support heart health.

Whole Grains

Replace refined grains with whole grains like brown rice, quinoa, oats, and whole wheat, as they are high in fiber and can help lower cholesterol levels.

Leafy Greens

Vegetables like spinach, kale, and collard greens are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that promote heart health.

Beriies

Blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries are excellent sources of antioxidants and fiber, which can reduce the risk of heart disease.
